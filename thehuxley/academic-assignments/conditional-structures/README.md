# Conditional Structures - Challenge Solutions

This directory contains solutions for programming challenges focused on decision-making structures hosted on [The Huxley](https://thehuxley.com/).

---

## Challenge List

The following challenges are classified as **Level 1** (Iniciante) and focus on conditional logic and branching.

| Folder | Challenge | Level | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **01-quadratic-equation** | Quadratic Equation (Equação do Segundo Grau) | ⭐ (1) | Calculates real roots ($ax^2 + bx + c = 0$) based on the delta value. |
| **02-championship** | Championship (Campeonato) | ⭐ (1) | Ranks football teams based on points (wins/draws) and goal difference. |
| **03-increasing-order** | Ascending Order (Ordem Crescente) | ⭐ (1) | Reads three integers and outputs them in ascending order. |
| **04-salary-increase** | Salary Increase (Reajuste Salarial) | ⭐ (1) | Applies specific percentage brackets (5%, 7%, or 10%) based on the salary. |
| **05-extended-warranty** | Extended Warranty (Garantia Estendida) | ⭐ (1) | Calculates product prices with added fees (0%, 3%, or 5%) based on the plan. |
| **06-student-performance** | Student Performance (Desempenho do Estudante) | ⭐ (1) | Calculates arithmetic mean and classifies status (Approved/Reproved). |
| **07-fly-fast-tax** | Fly Fast Baggage Tax (Taxa de Bagagem) | ⭐ (1) | Calculates baggage fees based on weight brackets or flags exceeded limits. |

---

### 🛠 Technologies and Concepts Practiced
These **Level 1** exercises focus on:
* If/Else Statements (**Estruturas de Decisão**).
* Comparison Operators (**Operadores de Comparação**): `>`, `<`, `==`, `!=`, `>=`, `<=`.
* Logical Operators (**Operadores Lógicos**): `AND`, `OR`, `NOT`.
* Mathematical handling of quadratic formulas ($\Delta = b^2 - 4ac$) and tax calculations.
