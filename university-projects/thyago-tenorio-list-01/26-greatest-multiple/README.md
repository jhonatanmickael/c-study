# Problem 06 - Repetition

## Problem Statement
Your goal is to determine the greatest multiple of a given integer that is less than or equal to another given integer.

---

## Example
* **Input 1 (Integer):** 7
* **Input 2 (Limit):** 30
* **Output:** 28
